Inter-Page Navigation II

  • In textual Web pages the concepts that can be hyperlinked have the following properties:

    • They are spatial (located in space on the screen) allowing mouse users to select links with a single action.

    • They can be quickly scanned in parallel by sighted users to reveal a kind of gestalt composed of the link number, order, location, grouping and density on a page.
