Signed Web Page Features: Link Bar

  • Provides the link gestalt information: the number, location, grouping and density of hyperlinks.

  • The link indicators in the link bar turn from blue to red when the linked concept is current.

  • Clicking on a link's representation in the link bar plays the linked time interval in the video.

Long Description: THe link density bar in gray with blue rectangles positionned at intervals along its length. One of the rectangles is red, signifying that this link is current. Next and previous link buttons follow.
