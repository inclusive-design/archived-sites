Sign Languages

  • There are numerous sign languages in use by deaf communities around the world:

    • ASL: American Sign Language

    • LSQ: Langue des Signes Québécoise

    • BSL: British Sign Language

    • …

  • These are separate languages as opposed to signing systems (such as ESE - Exact Signed English) that are based on spoken languages.
